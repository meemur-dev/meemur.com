Privacy policy

The short version: we collect as little as we can, we never sell your data, and you can email us anytime to see or delete what we hold.

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Who we are

meemur is a boutique digital agency founded and run by Onur Yanar. This policy covers meemur.com, which is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. For anything privacy-related, write to contact@meemur.com.

What we collect, and why

  • Contact form. Your name, email address, and message are delivered straight to our inbox as an email — we don't store them in a database. Submissions pass through a Cloudflare Turnstile spam check before they're sent.
  • Newsletter. If you subscribe in the footer, we receive your email address and keep it only to send you occasional tips. Unsubscribe anytime by emailing us or replying to any newsletter.
  • Analytics. We use Google Analytics 4 (loaded via Google Tag Manager) to understand how the site is used — pages visited, approximate location, and device type. We look at this data in aggregate; it doesn't tell us who you are.
  • Hosting and security. Cloudflare processes technical data such as IP addresses in server logs to serve the site and protect it from abuse.

Cookies

The only cookies we set ourselves come from Google Analytics, which uses them to recognize repeat visits. You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings, or opt out of Google Analytics entirely with Google's browser add-on. The site works fine without cookies.

Who we share data with

Nobody, beyond the services that run the site on our behalf: Cloudflare (hosting and spam protection), Google (analytics), and Resend (delivers form submissions to our inbox as email). We never sell your data, and this site runs no advertising trackers.

How long we keep it

Messages you send us are kept in our inbox like any business correspondence. Newsletter addresses are kept until you unsubscribe. Analytics data is retained by Google Analytics for up to 14 months, then deleted automatically.

Your rights

Wherever you are — including under the EU's GDPR and Türkiye's KVKK — you can ask us what data we hold about you, have it corrected or deleted, or object to how it's used. Email contact@meemur.com and we'll sort it out within a few days, no forms required.

Changes to this policy

If we change how the site handles data — say, a new analytics tool or a real mailing-list provider — we'll update this page and the date at the top.
